About

Hey, My name is Bujar Alimadhi. I'm a Web Developer, who is passionate about creating beautiful responsive websites and web apps that provide users the best and most appropriate experience suited to their device and browser. I really enjoy solving problems as well as making things pretty and easy to use, built with best practices. I have a special interest in use of animation on the web.

My background is a mixture of education. I hold a Graduation Certificate from "Link Academy" front-end development lab. I am an self - taught web developer. I learned a lot from the most popular, local and online courses, but I have learned most of my skills from just setting projects and doing new things.

I've used a wide range of different languages and frameworks, but I'm currently loving working with HTML, CSS/SCSS, and JavaScript/React on the front-end.

I also love learning new things, so I'm always happy for opportunities to try to use something I haven't before, or even to revisit something I haven't used in a while.

Outside of coding , I enjoy hiking , and travelling .
